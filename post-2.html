Ever since I went to the U.S. I have been a fan of Shake Shack and have longed to eat a burger from Shake Shack here in the Philippines. Now that there is one in Megamall, I tried the ShackBurger, which is just a simple quarter-pounder cheeseburger with some lettuce and tomato. It was amazing. I was a bit nervous since I first thought the quality might drop but it did not disappoint me at all. I would give this a 10/5 stars if I could.
